Dior - Egypt Fashion Show

Photography

Overview

In December 2022, PingMuse had the privilege of covering Dior's iconic fashion event in Egypt. This event celebrated Dior's 75th anniversary and featured a spectacular fashion show in the heart of the Giza desert, with the Great Pyramid of Giza as the backdrop. PingMuse's dedicated photographer,Mohamed Alaa, was on-site to capture the essence of this remarkable fashion extravaganza.

Our photographer Mohamed Alaa masterfully enchanted the runway, bringing to life Dior's exquisite designs against the awe-inspiring backdrop of the Great Pyramid of Giza. Each frame pulsates with the dance of contemporary fashion and the echoes of antiquity.

High-Profile Guests

The event drew luminaries from around the world, including South Korean sensation Sehun, the English actor Robert Pattinson, British model Lila Moss, and esteemed designers such as Stefano Pilati and the creative minds behind Berlin-based label GmbH.

Every stitch, fold, and texture of Dior's Fall collection became a work of art under the photographer's enchantment, inviting viewers to touch the magic through high-resolution imagery.

Dior's Egypt Fashion Show was not just an event; it was a transcendental experience, and PingMuse's photography has immortalized this enchantment.
